### range()
Generate a sequence of numbers
- `range(n)` → 0 to n-1
- `range(a, b)` → a to b-1
- `range(a, b, step)` → a to b-1 with step
- Arguments are separated by **commas**, not colons
- `range(0, 10)` ✓ / `range(0:10)` ✗
- Colons (`:`) are for slicing (`list[a:b]`), commas (`,`) are for function arguments

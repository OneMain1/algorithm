### rstrip() / lstrip() / strip()
Remove whitespace from strings
- `s.rstrip()` - remove trailing (right) whitespace
- `s.lstrip()` - remove leading (left) whitespace
- `s.strip()` - remove both sides
- **BOJ tip**: use `rstrip()` to fix format errors from trailing spaces
- Example: `'  *  '.rstrip()` → `'  *'`
